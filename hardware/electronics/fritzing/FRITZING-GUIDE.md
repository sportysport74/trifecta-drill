## FRITZING QUICK START STEPS
## ===========================

**Step 1:** Download Fritzing from https://fritzing.org/download/
**Step 2:** Install and open
**Step 3:** Drag "Arduino Mega 2560" from parts bin
**Step 4:** Add components around it following this guide
**Step 5:** Export to PDF for printing
